About Me

-Take time to do what makes your soul happy-

My name is Alexandru Adrian Cozma,i was born in Vicovu de Sus,Suceava,Romania.My passion for art starts since i've been in primary school i remember that day when i draw for first time and to my surprise i liked it.


In the following years i continue to draw and i realized i have talent, i've tried many techniques like Airbrush,Oil Paint,Pencils,but the one that i loved and and I felt it was for me is Colored Pencils.



Drawing is a good way to relax myself,it makes me to escape from problems of life and also gives me a good mood.
